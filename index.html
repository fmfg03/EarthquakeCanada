Conference Overview

The Joint Canadian Conference and Pacific Conference on Earthquake Engineering was held in Vancouver, British Columbia from June 25-30, 2023.

This international conference brought together researchers, practitioners, and experts to advance earthquake engineering and seismic risk reduction.
